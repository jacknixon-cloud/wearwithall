WearWithAll
Privacy Policy
Effective date: January 1, 2025

The Short Version

WearWithAll does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything the app does happens entirely on your device.

Camera

WearWithAll uses your device camera to analyze the colors of clothing items. Camera access is required for the app to function.

No photos or video are saved by the app. No images are transmitted to any server. Color analysis is performed locally on your device in real time.

History

Your most recent color and outfit results are stored locally on your device so you can refer back to them. This data never leaves your device and is permanently deleted if you uninstall the app.

Data We Do Not Collect

WearWithAll does not collect your name, email address, location, device identifiers, usage analytics, or any other personal information. There are no third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools in the app.

Third Parties

WearWithAll does not share any data with third parties because no data is collected in the first place.

The app is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain information as part of that distribution in accordance with their own privacy policy.

Children

WearWithAll does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. The app contains no features that require account creation or personal data of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date. We will not reduce your privacy protections without clear notice.
