Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the app need camera access?
The camera is how WearWithAll reads colors from your clothing. No photos are stored or sent anywhere — the analysis happens instantly and nothing leaves your device.
What's the difference between Small Eye and Big Eye?
Small Eye samples a tight point on your fabric, best for solid colors. Big Eye samples a wider area and picks up multiple colors at once, which works better for heathered, patterned, or mixed fabrics.
Why is the color identification sometimes off?
Lighting has a big effect on color accuracy. Try moving to a brighter, more evenly lit area and avoid strong colored light sources. The app will warn you when lighting looks too dim.
How does the Outfit Helper work?
Scan each clothing item one at a time, then tap Analyze. WearWithAll will tell you if the colors match, clash, or are neutral — and explain why in plain language.
How long does History keep my results?
Your 10 most recent Identify and Outfit checks are stored automatically. They are kept on your device only and are cleared if you delete the app.
